Work at MIPT

At MIPT, I was part of the Laboratory of Applied Computational Geophysics where I developed a way to model seismic response from facing fractures by using the Chimera grids approach. The results were presented at multiple conferences, such as:

  • Modeling of fractures using the Chimera grid approach Near Surface Geoscience Conference & Exhibition (Portugal, 2016)
  • Usage of hierarchical grids in seismic computations 50 years of grid-characteristic method’s development (MIPT, 2018)
